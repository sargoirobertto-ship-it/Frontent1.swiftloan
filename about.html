Our Mission

Swift Loan exists to democratize access to credit in Kenya by leveraging mobile technology to provide instant, fair, and transparent financial services to underserved communities.

We believe every Kenyan deserves access to financial opportunities that can help them build better lives, start businesses, handle emergencies, and achieve their dreams.

Instant loan decisions

Inclusion

Serving all Kenyans

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Swift Loan emerged from a simple observation: millions of Kenyans needed quick access to credit but were excluded from traditional banking systems due to lengthy processes, collateral requirements, and geographic barriers.

Our Journey

1

2024 - Foundation

Swift Loan was founded with a vision to bridge Kenya's credit gap using mobile technology.

2

2024 - M-Pesa Integration

Launched seamless M-Pesa integration for instant loan disbursement and repayment.

3

2024 - AI Credit Scoring

Implemented advanced AI algorithms for fair and accurate credit assessment.

2025 - National Expansion

Expanded services nationwide, serving customers in all 47 counties.

2025 - Present

Continuing to innovate with enhanced features and expanded loan products.
